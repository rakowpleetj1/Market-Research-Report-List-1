<p><p>The anti-static bag market comprises two main types: metal anti-static bags and non-metallic anti-static bags. Metal anti-static bags, often made from aluminum or similar materials, provide excellent shielding against electrostatic discharge (ESD) and are ideal for storing sensitive electronic components. Non-metallic anti-static bags, typically made from polyethylene or similar plastics, offer lightweight and cost-effective protection against ESD while being more flexible. Both types are essential for safeguarding electronic devices during storage and transportation, ensuring reliability and performance.</p></p>
