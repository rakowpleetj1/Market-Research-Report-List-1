<p><p>The 300mm 12-inch silicon wafer market encompasses various types of wafers essential for semiconductor manufacturing. Epitaxial wafers facilitate the growth of thin semiconductor layers, enhancing performance. Polished wafers provide a smooth surface for high-quality chip fabrication. Annealing wafers are utilized in thermal processes to modify material properties, improving yield and efficiency. SOI (Silicon-On-Insulator) wafers enable reduced parasitic capacitance, enhancing device performance, particularly in low-power applications. Together, these types support advancements in modern electronics and microelectronics.</p></p>
