<p><p>The portable spirometers market consists of two main types: mechanical spirometers and electronic spirometers. Mechanical spirometers operate using a bellows or turbine mechanism to measure airflow and volume, providing simplicity and reliability. Electronic spirometers, on the other hand, utilize advanced sensors and digital technology for precise measurements, enhanced data analysis, and connectivity with devices. Both types are essential for respiratory assessments, with increasing demand driven by the rise of home healthcare and remote monitoring solutions.</p></p>
