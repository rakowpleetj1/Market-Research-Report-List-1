<p><p>Tissue-based diagnostics encompass various techniques used for disease detection and characterization. Immunohistochemistry (IHC) employs antibodies to visualize specific protein expressions within tissue samples. In Situ Hybridization (ISH) detects specific nucleic acid sequences, aiding in gene expression analysis. Digital pathology integrates imaging and data management for enhanced diagnosis, enabling pathologists to analyze samples remotely. Workflow management software streamlines processes in labs, ensuring efficient sample handling. Special staining techniques highlight various tissue components, aiding in accurate diagnosis and research.</p></p>
