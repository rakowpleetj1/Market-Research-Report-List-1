<p><ul><li>Cloud-Based</li><li>On-Premise</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Advertisement blockers can be categorized into two primary market types: cloud-based and on-premise solutions. Cloud-based ad blockers operate through remote servers, filtering ads before they reach the user's device, offering ease of use and automatic updates. On-premise ad blockers, installed directly on users' devices or networks, provide more control and potentially increased privacy, but require manual updates and maintenance. Each approach caters to different user preferences and needs, balancing convenience and security in managing online advertisements.</p></p>
