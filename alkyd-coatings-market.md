<p><ul><li>Non-drying Coatings</li><li>Drying Coatings</li><li>Semi-drying Coatings</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The alkyd coatings market is categorized into three types: non-drying, drying, and semi-drying coatings. Non-drying coatings remain flexible and do not harden over time, making them suitable for temporary applications. Drying coatings undergo a curing process, forming a hard film ideal for durable finishes. Semi-drying coatings strike a balance, partially curing to provide good adhesion and flexibility, often used in industrial applications. Each type serves distinct purposes based on the desired performance and application requirements.</p></p>
