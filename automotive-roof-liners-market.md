<p><p>The automotive roof liners market comprises primarily two types: thermoplastics and thermosets. </p><p>Thermoplastic roof liners are lightweight, flexible materials that can be easily molded and recycled, offering cost-effectiveness and design versatility. They are ideal for high-volume production. </p><p>Thermoset roof liners, on the other hand, are known for their durability and heat resistance. They undergo a chemical change during curing, resulting in a stable structure that provides excellent insulation and sound-damping qualities. Both types cater to varying automotive design and performance requirements.</p></p>
