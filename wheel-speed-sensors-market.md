<p><p>Wheel speed sensors are essential components in automotive systems, primarily used for monitoring wheel rotation speed. The market can be categorized into two main types: Magnetic Electric Type and Hall Effect Type. </p><p>Magnetic Electric Type sensors utilize a magnetic field and a rotating wheel's movement to generate an electrical signal. In contrast, Hall Effect Type sensors employ a semiconductor device to detect the presence of a magnetic field, providing precise wheel speed measurements. Both types are integral for stability control, anti-lock braking systems, and overall vehicle safety.</p></p>
