<p><p>The IT service software market is divided into two main types: on-premises and cloud-based solutions. On-premises software is installed locally on a company's servers and requires significant upfront investment in hardware and infrastructure, providing full control over data management and security. In contrast, cloud-based software is hosted on remote servers and accessed via the internet, offering flexibility, scalability, and lower initial costs, while simplifying updates and maintenance, making it a popular choice for businesses of all sizes.</p></p>
