<p><p>The fueling nozzle market comprises two main types: automatic shut-off nozzles and manual nozzles. Automatic shut-off nozzles enhance safety and efficiency by automatically stopping fuel flow when the tank is full, minimizing spills and overflows. In contrast, manual nozzles require operators to control the fuel flow, providing flexibility but requiring more user attention to prevent overfilling. Both types cater to varying customer preferences and operational needs within the fueling industry, impacting their adoption rates and market dynamics.</p></p>
