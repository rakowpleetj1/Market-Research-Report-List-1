<p><p>The cardiology software market is divided into two primary types: on-premises and cloud-based solutions. On-premises software is installed directly on a healthcare facility's servers, offering greater control over data security and customization but requiring significant upfront investments and maintenance. In contrast, cloud-based software is hosted on external servers, providing scalability, easy access from multiple locations, and reduced IT burden. This model often features subscription pricing, making it more cost-effective for many healthcare providers while ensuring regular updates and support.</p></p>
