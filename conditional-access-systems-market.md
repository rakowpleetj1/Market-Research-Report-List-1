<p><p>Conditional Access Systems (CAS) are essential for controlling access to digital content. The Smartcard CAS market relies on physical smartcards that store decryption keys, ensuring secure content delivery. This traditional approach is widely used in pay-TV and satellite services. In contrast, Cardless CAS utilizes software-based solutions, allowing access to content without physical cards. This method is increasingly popular due to its flexibility and ease of integration with various devices, catering to the growing demand for digital streaming services.</p></p>
