<p><p>The power supply market can be categorized into two main types: AC-DC and DC-DC. AC-DC converters transform alternating current (AC) from the grid into direct current (DC) for devices like computers and LED lighting. Conversely, DC-DC converters take an existing DC input and convert it to a different voltage level, commonly used in battery-powered devices and renewable energy systems. Both markets cater to a variety of applications, ensuring efficient power management and compatibility with diverse electronic systems.</p></p>
