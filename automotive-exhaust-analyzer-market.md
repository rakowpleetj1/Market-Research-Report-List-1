<p><strong>The Automotive Exhaust Analyzer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Non-Dispersive Infrared</li><li>Flame Ionization Detector</li><li>Chemiluminescence Analyser</li><li>Constant Volume Sampler</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive exhaust analyzer market includes various types of technologies for measuring emissions. Non-Dispersive Infrared (NDIR) analyzers detect gases by measuring absorbed infrared light. Flame Ionization Detectors (FID) use a flame to ionize hydrocarbons, providing sensitive measurements of organic compounds. Chemiluminescence Analyzers measure nitrogen oxides through chemiluminescent reactions. Constant Volume Samplers collect exhaust samples at a consistent volume, allowing for accurate analysis of pollutants. Each type serves different applications in vehicle emissions testing and compliance.</p></p>
