<p><p>The restaurant POS terminals market comprises two main types: fixed and mobile POS terminals. Fixed POS terminals are stationary devices typically installed at checkout counters, enabling streamlined transactions and inventory management. They offer robust features for handling high-traffic environments. Conversely, mobile POS terminals are portable devices that facilitate transactions anywhere in the restaurant, improving customer service and reducing wait times. This flexibility allows staff to engage directly with customers, enhancing the overall dining experience and operational efficiency.</p></p>
