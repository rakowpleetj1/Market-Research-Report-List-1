<p><p>The passenger vehicle radiator market comprises primarily two types: aluminum radiators and copper radiators. Aluminum radiators are lightweight, corrosion-resistant, and offer efficient heat dissipation, making them popular in modern vehicles. They are often more cost-effective and have a longer lifespan. Copper radiators, on the other hand, provide excellent thermal conductivity and durability but are heavier and typically more expensive. Both types play crucial roles in engine cooling, directly impacting vehicle performance and longevity.</p></p>
