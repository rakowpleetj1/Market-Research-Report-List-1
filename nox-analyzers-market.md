<p><p>The NOx analyzers market features various types, including Chemiluminescence Detection (CLD) and Direct Absorption Spectroscopy. CLD measures nitrogen oxides by reacting them with ozone, producing light that is quantified to determine NOx levels, making it highly sensitive and widely used in regulatory compliance. In contrast, Direct Absorption Spectroscopy utilizes the absorption characteristics of NOx gases in specific wavelengths, offering a non-intrusive method that allows real-time monitoring. Both methods serve critical roles in environmental monitoring and air quality management.</p></p>
