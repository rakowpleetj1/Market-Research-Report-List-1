<p><strong>The HVAC Temperature Sensor for Commercial Buildings Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>NTC Temperature Sensor</li><li>PTC Temperature Sensor</li><li>RTD Temperature Sensor</li><li>Thermocouples (TCs) Temperature Sensor</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>In the HVAC temperature sensor market for commercial buildings, key types include:</p><p>1. **NTC Temperature Sensor**: Based on negative temperature coefficient thermistors, these sensors decrease resistance as temperature rises, commonly used for accurate indoor climate control.</p><p>2. **PTC Temperature Sensor**: Utilizing positive temperature coefficient materials, these sensors increase resistance with temperature, often found in overheating protection.</p><p>3. **RTD Temperature Sensor**: Resistance Temperature Detectors provide precise measurements by correlating temperature to resistance change, ideal for industrial applications.</p><p>4. **Thermocouples (TCs)**: Comprising two dissimilar metals, they generate voltage proportional to temperature difference, suitable for extreme conditions.</p><p>5. **Other**: This category includes various emerging technologies and niche sensors tailored for specific HVAC applications.</p></p>
