<p><p>The Fixed Refrigerant Leak Detectors market is primarily divided into two types: Halide and Electronic detectors. Halide detectors utilize halogen gas, which reacts to refrigerants, providing a visual indication of leaks through a change in flame color. In contrast, Electronic detectors employ sensors to identify refrigerant presence, offering more precise detection and often featuring digital displays. Both types serve to monitor refrigerant levels in systems, enhancing safety and efficiency in HVAC applications by preventing environmental damage and reducing operational costs.</p></p>
