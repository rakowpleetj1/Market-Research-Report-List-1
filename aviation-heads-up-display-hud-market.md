<p><p>The aviation heads-up display (HUD) market is primarily divided into two types: conventional HUD and augmented reality (AR)-based HUD. Conventional HUDs project essential flight data onto the cockpit windshield, enabling pilots to maintain situational awareness without looking down at instruments. In contrast, AR-based HUDs enhance this experience by overlaying digital information onto real-world views, providing more immersive and context-rich data. While conventional HUDs are widely used, AR-based HUDs are gaining traction due to their advanced capabilities and improved user experience.</p></p>
