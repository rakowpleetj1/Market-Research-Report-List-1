<p><p>The ophthalmic medicine market encompasses various forms of treatments for eye disorders. Gelling agents enhance the viscosity of solutions for better adherence to the ocular surface. Tablets provide systemic treatment for underlying conditions, while injections deliver medication directly into the eye for conditions requiring rapid intervention or high drug concentration. Each type serves specific therapeutic needs.</p></p>
