<p><ul><li>Suspension Type</li><li>Roller Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The vacuum metallizing equipment market offers two primary types: suspension type and roller type. Suspension type equipment involves vaporizing metal in a controlled environment, enabling uniform coating on complex shapes and surfaces, ideal for intricate designs. Roller type equipment, on the other hand, utilizes a continuous process with rollers to apply the metal coating on flexible materials, making it efficient for mass production. Both types cater to different applications, enhancing the aesthetic and functional properties of various products.</p></p>
