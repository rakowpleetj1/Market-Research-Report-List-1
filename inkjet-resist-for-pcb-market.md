<p><p>Inkjet resist for the PCB market includes two primary types: UV Curable Inkjet Resists and Thermal Curable Inkjet Resists. UV Curable Inkjet Resists cure quickly when exposed to UV light, offering high-resolution patterns and excellent adhesion, making them ideal for intricate designs. On the other hand, Thermal Curable Inkjet Resists cure through heat exposure, providing enhanced durability and thermal stability, suitable for applications requiring robust performance. Both types cater to the growing demand for efficient and precise PCB manufacturing processes.</p></p>
