<p><p>Scalping cutters are specialized tools used in machining for producing smooth, precise surfaces. There are two primary market types: straight and spiral. Straight scalping cutters feature parallel cutting edges, making them suitable for specific applications requiring direct cutting. In contrast, spiral scalping cutters have cutting edges twisted around the body, enhancing chip removal and reducing friction, which allows for smoother operation and improved material flow. Both types cater to different machining needs, offering efficiency and precision in various industrial applications.</p></p>
