<p><p>The ZigBee Home Automation market features two main device types: Full Function Devices (FFDs) and Reduced Function Endpoint Devices (RFDs). FFDs are robust devices capable of performing various roles such as coordinators and routers, enabling complex networks and greater interoperability among units. In contrast, RFDs are simpler, lower-cost devices designed for specific functions, suitable for applications like sensors and remotes. This differentiation allows for flexible, scalable home automation solutions, catering to varying user needs and budgets within smart home ecosystems.</p></p>
