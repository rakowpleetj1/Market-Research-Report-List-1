<p><strong>The Direct ToF Cameras Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Pulse-Based ToF Cameras</li><li>Continuous-Wave (CW) ToF Cameras</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Direct Time-of-Flight (ToF) cameras are classified into two main types: Pulse-Based ToF Cameras and Continuous-Wave (CW) ToF Cameras. Pulse-Based ToF Cameras emit short light pulses and measure the time it takes for the light to return, providing precise depth measurements. In contrast, Continuous-Wave ToF Cameras emit a continuous light signal and determine distance based on phase shift. Both technologies serve applications in robotics, automotive, and consumer electronics, enhancing capabilities in depth sensing and object detection.</p></p>
