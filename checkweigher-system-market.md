<p><strong>The Checkweigher System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>In-Motion Checkweighers</li><li>Intermittent Checkweighers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The checkweigher system market is primarily divided into two types: in-motion checkweighers and intermittent checkweighers. In-motion checkweighers operate continuously, measuring products as they move along a conveyor belt, allowing for high-speed processing without halting production. In contrast, intermittent checkweighers pause the product during weighing, ensuring precise measurement but often at a slower pace. Each type serves distinct operational needs, with in-motion checkweighers favored for high throughput environments, while intermittent systems provide enhanced accuracy for sensitive applications.</p></p>
