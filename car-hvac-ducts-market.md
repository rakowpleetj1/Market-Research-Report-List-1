<p><p>The Car HVAC Ducts market is primarily divided into two types: gas ducts and liquid ducts. Gas ducts facilitate the circulation of heated or cooled air within the vehicle, ensuring optimal cabin climate control. They typically use a network of vents to distribute air evenly. In contrast, liquid ducts are designed for systems that rely on fluid-based heating or cooling, such as coolant circulation. Both types play critical roles in maintaining passenger comfort and enhancing overall vehicle performance.</p></p>
