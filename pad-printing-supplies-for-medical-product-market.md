<p><ul><li>Solvent-based</li><li>UV Curing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Pad printing supplies for the medical product market primarily encompass two main types: solvent-based and UV curing inks. Solvent-based inks utilize solvents to dissolve pigments, offering excellent adhesion and vibrant colors on various surfaces, making them suitable for medical devices. Conversely, UV curing inks are quick-drying and beneficial for achieving sharp details while ensuring safety by minimizing volatile organic compounds (VOCs). Both types cater to stringent regulatory standards, ensuring reliable marking and branding for medical products.</p></p>
