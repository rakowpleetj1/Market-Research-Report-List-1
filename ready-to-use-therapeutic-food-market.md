<p><p>The Ready-to-use Therapeutic Food market encompasses three main types: solid, semi-solid paste, and drinkable therapeutic food. Solid forms, like energy-dense bars or biscuits, provide convenient nutrition for those in need. Semi-solid pastes, often used for young children, offer easy consumption and palatability while delivering essential nutrients. Drinkable therapeutic foods are designed for individuals with swallowing difficulties, ensuring a smooth texture and high nutrient content. Each type is tailored to meet specific dietary needs in therapeutic settings.</p></p>
