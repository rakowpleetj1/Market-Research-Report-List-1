<p><p>The implantable pacemaker market includes various devices designed to regulate heart rhythms. Traditional pacemakers ensure steady heartbeats, while implantable cardioverters-defibrillators (ICDs) monitor and correct life-threatening arrhythmias via shocks. Biventricular ICDs, a specific subset, provide pacing to both heart ventricles, improving heart function in patients with heart failure. Together, these devices address a range of cardiac conditions, enhancing patient quality of life and reducing the risk of severe complications associated with arrhythmias and heart failure.</p></p>
