<p><p>The Endovascular Stent Grafts market is categorized into three primary types: synthetic, biological, and biosynthetic. </p><p>Synthetic stent grafts, typically made from materials like polyester or polytetrafluoroethylene, offer durability and resistance to degradation. </p><p>Biological stent grafts use natural tissues, promoting better integration with the body and reducing rejection risks. </p><p>Biosynthetic stent grafts combine synthetic and biological elements, aiming to enhance biocompatibility while maintaining structural integrity. This diversification caters to varying patient needs and conditions in vascular interventions.</p></p>
