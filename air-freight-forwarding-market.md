<p><strong>The Air Freight Forwarding Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Full Charter</li><li>Split Charter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Air freight forwarding markets are categorized primarily into full charter and split charter segments. In a full charter market, an entire aircraft is booked for transporting goods, providing dedicated capacity and flexibility for large shipments. Conversely, the split charter market involves sharing aircraft space among multiple clients, allowing for cost-effective solutions for smaller shipments. Both markets cater to varying shipping needs, with the full charter offering exclusivity and speed, while split charters enhance efficiency and reduce shipping expenses.</p></p>
