<p><p>The lithographic chemicals market encompasses various categories essential for semiconductor manufacturing. Removers facilitate the cleaning of substrates by dissolving unwanted materials. Deposition precursors are chemicals used to create thin films during the deposition process. Etchants selectively remove material to define patterns on wafers, while dopants introduce impurities to alter electrical properties. Electron beam resists (Esists) are specialized materials for electron beam lithography. The "Others" category includes additional chemicals that support various lithographic processes and applications.</p></p>
